LockWidgets

Available on Dynastic Repo

LockWidgets is a free and open source tweak that allows you to place a widget from any application on your lock screen, meaning you no longer have to swipe across to see it in with other widgets. Created with EvenDev.

Works on iOS 11 and higher

Pastel

Available on Dynastic Repo

Pastel is a free and open source tweak to add colour to your notification badges. Pastel will automatically select the most prominent colour in the app icon and it will set the badge colour to it! With Pastel, you can make your Home Screen look fluid and clean...

Tested on iOS 12 and higher

Lyricify

Available on Packix Repo

Lyricify is a paid tweak that brings the Apple Music Lyric function to your Springboard, it adds supports for streaming services like Spotify with the help of the Ksoft API.

Works on iOS 12.2 and higher

Dragspring

Available on Packix Repo

Dragspring is a free tweak that adds new functionality (i.e. subtitle text) and better support for newer iOS versions to the original tweak, PullToRespring.

Recommended to use iOS 11 and higher

ModernPower

Available on Packix Repo

ModernPower is a free tweak that redesigns the iOS Power Down menu with more useful featuresand a beautiful and non-intrusive design.

Works on iOS 11 and 12

Mitsuha Infinity

Discontinued

Mitsuha Infinity is the original audio visualiser tweak by NepetaDev and 0xc0ldra1n with updates for iOS 13 and new versions of Spotify, it was supposed to be replaced by Mitsuha Forever in the future.

Works on iOS 11 and higher

HoneyBadger
