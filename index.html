LockWidgets

Available on Dynastic Repo

LockWidgets is a free and open source tweak that allows you to place a widget from any application on your lock screen, meaning you no longer have to swipe across to see it in with other widgets. Created with EvenDev. Works on iOS 11 and higher

Lyricify

Coming soon...

Lyricify is a free tweak that brings the Apple Music Lyric function to your Springboard, it adds supports for streaming services like Spotify with the help of the Ksoft API. Tested on iOS 12 and higher

Dragspring

Available on Packix Repo

Dragspring is a free tweak that adds new functionality (i.e. subtitle text) and better support for newer iOS versions to the original tweak, PullToRespring. Recommended to be used iOS 11 and higher!

ModernPower

Available on Packix Repo

ModernPower is a free tweak that redesigns the iOS Power Down menu with more useful featuresand a beautiful and non-intrusive design. Works on iOS 11 -> 12

Mitsuha Infinity

Available on my repo

Mitsuha Infinity is the original audio visualiser tweak by NepetaDev and 0xc0ldra1n with updates for iOS 13 and new versions of Spotify, it is being replaced by Mitsuha Forever sometime in the future. Works on iOS 11 -> 13.

Chrono

Discontinued

Chrono was a free and open source tweak that added seconds to the world clock view in the iOS Clock App. You can view the code here. Works on iOS 11 -> 12
